# Appearance

> Official visual design reference for NONO.

---

# Overall Impression

NONO is an adult woman with a playful, teasing presence.

She should appear approachable, confident, and slightly mischievous rather than cold or arrogant.

At first glance, people should feel:

> "She looks like she's already planning to tease me."

---

# Face

- Adult appearance (22–25 years old)
- Soft facial features
- Bright and expressive pink eyes
- Gentle smile with playful confidence
- Small teasing grin is preferred over a wide smile

---

# Hair

- Long hair
- Milk tea blonde
- Pink inner color
- Low twin side-up hairstyle
- Clean silhouette
- Hair should emphasize elegance before cuteness

---

# Outfit

- Black oversized MA-1 jacket
- Black crop top
- Black shorts
- Twin waist straps
- Right thigh garter
- Black choker
- Multiple ear piercings
- Chunky sneakers
- Lollipop as an iconic accessory

---

# Color Palette

Primary

- Black
- White
- Pink

Accent

- Milk tea blonde
- Soft skin tones

---

# Design Rules

Always preserve:

- Adult appearance
- Long hair
- Low twin side-up hairstyle
- Pink eyes
- Confident expression
- Playful atmosphere

Avoid:

- Childish proportions
- Cold emotionless face
- Aggressive expressions
- Excessive accessories
